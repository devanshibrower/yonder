One number becomes a moon

Drag the green dot around the wheel. Watch its side-to-side position (that's cosine) turn into the width of the moon's day/night line. It's all one motion.

Why dates need a library, not a stopwatch

The moon's orbit is an ellipse, so it speeds up when close and slows when far. Equal days are not equal phase. Drag day by day and watch the spacing stay uneven, that drift is why we let suncalc do the real math.
